  • The Developers
Destiny 2, and its predecessor Destiny, is developed by the Seattle based company Bungie Inc. Bungie have also developed Myth, Oni and Halo before begining work on the 2014 release of the original Destiny. Until early 2019 Destiny was being produced by Activision; however, since then bungie have produced the game themselves. They pride themselves on having a very diverse development team and have run a number of sales for appropriate charities. One example would be the ongoing sales on the Bungie Store where there are number of items that will have all proceeds sent directly to The Bungie Foundation, a charity run by Bungie that is centred around helping at risk children. One of the programs that The Bungie Foundation runs is called iPads for Kids and directly supports children in hospitals by providing them with an iPad for entertainment and education. As part of their diversity they have also recently established internal committees called Black at Bungie and Trans at Bungie to help support their employees of minority groups.
